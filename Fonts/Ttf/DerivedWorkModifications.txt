The present folder contains a subset of the New Computer Modern fonts in ttf 
format. Most fonts in the present folder are a mere conversion to ttf and have not
been modified.

Exceptions are the NewCMMathRadicalPieGreek-Bold, and 
NewCMMathRadicalPieGreek-Book files, that contain glyphs extracted from 
NewCMMath-Book and NewCMMath-Bold at different codepoints. 

Exact modification instructions can be found in Convert.py located one folder up
the present file.

You can download a copy of the original in the following address:
https://ctan.org/pkg/newcomputermodern
